SCHOOL COURSE SYLLABUS



INTRODUCTION TO BIOLOGY (BIO101)

COURSE DESCRIPTION

Introduction to Biology is a foundational course designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of the fundamental principles of biology. This course will explore the structure, function, and diversity of living organisms, as well as their interactions with the environment. Students will gain insights into cell biology, genetics, evolution, ecology, and more, fostering an appreciation for the complexity and beauty oof life on Earth.

LEARNING OBJECTIVES

Upon successful completion of this course, students will be able to:

  1. Explain the basic principles of biology and scientific methods.
  2. Describe the structure and function of cells and major cellular processes.
  3. Understand the principles of genetics and inheritance
  4. Analyze the mechanisms of evolution and natural selection.
  5. Comprehend the interrelationships within ecosystems and ecological systems.
